Our Story

A Decade of
Crafted Spaces.

Aura & Onyx was born in 2014 from a single, radical conviction — that luxury is not a price point, but a standard of intention. Our founders, frustrated by interior design firms that prioritised trend over truth, set out to build something different: an atelier where every decision is made with the weight of permanence in mind.

Over the past decade, we have transformed penthouses in Manhattan, coastal retreats in Malibu, and executive suites in Mayfair. Each project is approached not as a commission, but as a collaboration — a conversation between what the space demands and what the client desires.

We source materials globally. We work with master craftsmen. We refuse to compromise.

The Signature Aesthetic

Rich. Restrained.
Remarkable.

The Aura & Onyx aesthetic sits at the intersection of modern minimalism and sensory richness. We layer dark stone against soft linen. We pair raw brass with hand-stitched velvet. We place a single sculptural object where a lesser designer would crowd five.

Our palette draws from the earth — deep onyx, warm sand, burnished gold, the grey-green of aged bronze. Our materials are chosen for how they age, how they feel underfoot, how they catch afternoon light in February.

The result is interiors that feel inevitable — as though they could not have been any other way.

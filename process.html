Methodology

From Concept
to Keys.

A masterpiece is not created by accident. It is the result of a rigorous, obsessive, and entirely transparent process.

01

Phase One

Discovery &
Alignment

We begin by understanding the unspoken requirements of your life. This isn't merely a discussion of colour palettes or fabric preferences; it is a deep dive into how you move, how you entertain, and what brings you a sense of absolute peace. We audit your existing space, review architectural constraints, and establish a foundational budget and timeline.

Phase Two

Conceptual
Architecture

With the parameters set, our atelier begins drafting the spatial narrative. We present comprehensive mood boards, material trays featuring physical samples of stone and textile, and photorealistic 3D renderings. You won't have to imagine what your space will look like — you will see it, feel it, and understand exactly how the light will hit the marble at 4 PM in November.

02
03

Phase Three

Procurement &
Craftsmanship

Once the design is approved, we initiate the build and acquisition phase. We coordinate exclusively with master contractors to handle structural shifts. Simultaneously, our procurement team begins commissioning bespoke furniture, sourcing rare antiques from auction houses, and importing raw materials globally. We handle all logistics, quality control, and secure storage.

Phase Four

Installation &
The Reveal

The culmination of our methodology. Over a period of days or weeks, our team executes a flawless, white-glove installation. We place the furniture, hang the curated art, steam the drapery, and style the surfaces down to the placement of the last coffee table book. You simply hand us the keys to an empty site, and return to an immaculate, fully realised home.

Transparency is the foundation of every successful collaboration. Below are the parameters of how we operate.

We are entirely transparent about the investment required to achieve the Aura & Onyx standard. Residential engagements begin at $50,000, and commercial projects begin at $120,000. These minimums ensure we never have to compromise on materials, craftsmanship, or the time required to perfect your space.

Yes. While our atelier is based in the Design District, we operate globally. We have completed projects in 28 countries, including private estates in Lake Como, penthouses in Manhattan, and commercial spaces in Geneva. Our logistics team handles all international freight, customs, and import duties for bespoke furniture.

A standard full-home transformation takes between 6 to 14 months from the initial Discovery Call to the final White-Glove Reveal. This timeline accounts for structural architectural changes, contractor availability, and the 12-to-16 week lead times required to manufacture custom furniture in our European ateliers.

While we strongly prefer to utilize our own network of master craftsmen to guarantee our quality standard, we are willing to collaborate with vetted third-party architects and contractors. However, Aura & Onyx must retain full creative direction and oversight of the spatial execution.

We charge a flat spatial design fee for the conceptual architecture phase, which is determined after the initial site audit. Following that, we operate on a clear, fixed-percentage procurement model for all furnishings, materials, and contractor management. There are no hidden fees.

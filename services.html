Residential Design

Your home is the most intimate space you will ever inhabit. We approach every residential project with the understanding that we are not designing rooms — we are designing the backdrop of your life. From a single statement room to a complete multi-storey transformation, our residential service is comprehensive, considered, and entirely bespoke.

  • Full home renovations & luxury restyling
  • Single-room transformations
  • New build interior specification
  • Furniture selection & custom commissioning
  • Art curation & object placement

Commercial Spaces

A commercial space communicates your brand before a single word is spoken. We design environments that do the heavy lifting — environments that tell your clients, your guests, and your staff exactly who you are and what you stand for. From intimate boutique hotels to sweeping corporate headquarters, we bring the same obsessive precision to every square metre.

  • Boutique hotels & luxury hospitality

03

Bespoke Furniture Sourcing

The furniture in a truly exceptional interior is never generic and never accidental. Through our global network of master craftsmen, heritage ateliers, and design houses, we source and commission pieces that cannot be found in any showroom. Each acquisition is made with the specific project in mind — the exact weight, scale, material, and finish that the space demands.

  • Custom-built pieces from European ateliers
  • Vintage & antique acquisition
  • Designer collaboration & commissioning
  • Auction house representation
  • Fabric & upholstery specification
Enquire About This Service →
04

Spatial Planning

Before any material is chosen or any furniture selected, the space itself must be understood. Our spatial planning service examines the architectural bones of your environment — the flow of movement, the behaviour of light through the seasons, the acoustic properties, the relationship between rooms. We then build a spatial strategy that makes everything that follows feel inevitable.

  • Architectural flow analysis
  • Natural & artificial lighting design
  • Acoustic planning & treatment
  • 3D spatial modelling & rendering
  • Contractor coordination & oversight
